
# User Stories - EquiTix

## General User Stories
- **Secure Login:** As a user, I can log in with my email and password if my account has been previously activated.
- **Account Registration:** As a new user, I can sign up with my name, email, and a password (including an option for auto-generation).
- **Activation Gate:** As a new user, I understand that my account must be activated by an administrator before I can log in and make purchases.
- **Password Reset:** As a user who has forgotten their password, I can request a reset link to be sent to my email.

## Customer User Stories
- **Personal Dashboard:** As a logged-in user, I want a personal dashboard that shows my account details, membership status, and full purchase history.
- **Ethical Purchase:** As a fan, I want to purchase tickets and see the specific donation amount added to my transaction history.
- **Transparency:** As a fan, I want to see a chart of how the price will drop over time so I can decide whether to buy now or risk waiting.
- **Price Alert:** As a bargain hunter, I want to set a "Price Alert" so I don't miss the moment the ticket becomes affordable for me.
- **Tax Receipt:** As a donor, I want to download a tax-deductible receipt for the donation portion of my ticket purchase directly from my profile.

## Artist User Stories
- **Brand Protection:** As an artist, I want to set high initial prices to prevent scalpers from flipping my tickets for profit.
- **Philanthropy:** As an artist, I want to support specific causes and see the live impact generated by my tour through the global counter.

## Administration User Stories
- **User Activation (Manual Override):** As an admin, I can view a list of all registered users and manually activate or deactivate their accounts.
- **System Monitoring:** As an admin, I want to monitor all registered users and their purchase velocity to ensure the platform is functioning correctly.
- **Financial Integrity:** As an admin, I want to ensure only verified, activated accounts can process high-value ethical ticket transactions.
